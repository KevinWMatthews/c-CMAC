Shift the bits in an array to the left by one.

So:
 0xffee..1100
   ||      -- LSB
   -- MSB
is stored as:
 00 11 .. ee ff
Bitshifting:
 0x00        0x11        0xX     0xee        0xff
 0b0000 0000 0b0001 0001 0bX .. 0b1110 1110 0b1111 1111
yields:
 0x00        0x22               0xdd        0xfe
 0b0000 0000 0b0010 001X .. 0b1 0b1101 1101 0b1111 1110
                                                      - This bit wraps around.

    One byte:

bit shitfing 0 has no effect:
    0000 0000
    0000 0000

bit shift 1:
    0000 0001
    0000 0010

wrap around:
    1000 0000
    0000 0001

don't lose any bits:
    1111 1111
    1111 1111

    Two-bytes:

bit shitfing 0 has no effect:
    0000 0000 0000 0000
    0000 0000 0000 0000

bit shift 1:
    0000 0000 0000 0001
    0000 0000 0000 0010

shift across bytes:
    0000 0000 1000 0000
    0000 0001 0000 0000

wrap around:
    1000 0000 0000 0000
    0000 0000 0000 0001

don't lose any bits:
    1111 1111 1111 1111
    1111 1111 1111 1111
